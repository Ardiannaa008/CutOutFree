Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

🔒 The short version: your photos never leave your device. CutOut has no server-side image processing, no upload endpoint, and no database.

How image processing works

CutOut uses an AI model that runs entirely inside your browser via WebAssembly (powered by the open-source @imgly/background-removal library). When you upload or paste an image, it is processed locally on your own device. It is never transmitted to CutOut's servers or to any third party.

What we collect

  • We do not collect, store, or have access to your images.
  • We do not require an account, login, or email address.
  • We do not use cookies to track you across other sites.
  • Your browser caches the AI model locally (via localStorage) purely so it doesn't have to re-download it on your next visit. This is a technical cache, not personal data.

Hosting & basic analytics

This site is hosted on Vercel. Like most web hosts, Vercel's infrastructure may log standard technical data (such as IP address and request timestamps) for security and performance purposes. We do not layer any additional tracking or analytics on top of this.

Third-party links

If this page links to external sites (such as the background-removal library above), those sites have their own privacy practices, which we don't control.

Changes to this policy

If how this site works ever changes in a way that affects your privacy, this page will be updated to reflect it.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the contact details listed on the CutOut homepage.
